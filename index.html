    Welcome to Sunshine Butterfly

    Sunshine Butterfly is a non-profit group working on BL (Boys Love) manga, manhwa and manhua. We do not accept donations or payments, so please support the creators by purchasing the raws on the official platforms, such as Ridibooks, Bomtoon, etc. You can learn about getting an account and adult verification at some of these sites using the Buyer's Tutorial link in the top menu. If the site you'd like to purchase from is not available there, you're welcome to ask us in our Discord server and we'll do our best to guide you!

    Before You Proceed

    1. DO NOT share this website or any projects, chapters or screenshots containing unofficial translations publicly. Especially on any social media platforms such as TikTok, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, etc.
