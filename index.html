Welcome to Sunshine Butterfly

Sunshine Butterfly is a strictly non-profit group working on BL (Boys Love) manga, manhwa and manhua. We do not accept donations or payments, so please support the author and/or artist by purchasing the raws at the official platforms such as Ridibooks, Bomtoon, etc. You can learn about getting an account and adult verification on these sites using the Buyer's Tutorial link in the top menu. If the site you're purchasing from is not available there, you're welcome to ask us in our Discord server and we'll do our best to guide you!

The best way you can support us is to join the team!!! Refer to the recruitment channel of our Discord server for details. Korean Translators please we need you~~

Before You Proceed

  1. DO NOT share this website or any projects, chapters or screenshots containing unofficial translations publicly. Especially on any social media platforms such as TikTok, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, etc.
  2. DO NOT engage with the publisher, author or artist unless you have purchased from the official sources and do not reference any material taken from the unofficial translations.
  3. DO NOT repost our translations under any circumstances. Certain titles or updates may only be posted on this website while most will be made available on MangaDex with slight delay.
  4. Please take the age-rated warnings and trigger warnings seriously.
  5. Once an official English translation is available, we will cease to work on that series. Please support the author by purchasing the official English chapters.
  6. If images do not load, you might be in a location which is restricted from accessing the image server. Please use a VPN and try again. If that doesn't work, kindly reach out to us on Discord.
